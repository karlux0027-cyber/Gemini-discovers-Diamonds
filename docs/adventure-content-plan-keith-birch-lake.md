# Adventure content plan — Keith & family (Birch Lake)

Locked decisions from the creative check-in. Use this as the single source for shoot planning and briefs.

**Thursday Palm Springs demo:** While the family is visiting, the priority is showing **how** content gets created and published—not impressing them with a normal finished video. See [`thursday-demo-scope.md`](thursday-demo-scope.md) for the one-sentence promise and minimum demo bar.

## Talent & context

- **Keith:** Early 40s; two sons and a younger daughter; family-forward energy.
- **Place:** Northern Minnesota — Hackensack area, **Birch Lake** (dock, fish house, outdoor north-woods vibe).

## Creative lock-in

- **Format:** Mystery **box unboxing** with the kids fully involved — not sideline observers.
- **Locations (Q1):** **Both** — unbox on the **dock** *and* inside the **fish house** (use both for variety and story beats).
- **Gear focus (Q2):** **Full mix** — **fishing** (rods, lures, related tackle) **and** **hunting-oriented** items (e.g. camo/accessories as discussed). Treat as one blended “outdoors mystery” box, not fishing-only.
- **Kids’ role (Q3):** **They do it all** — open boxes, react, **test mystery bait on camera**, and participate in the full arc.
- **Vehicle beat:** Kids can **haul gear up** on a **snowmobile** or **four-wheeler** (treat as a signature transition / “adventure” moment — plan coverage and safety accordingly).

## Production notes

- **Audio:** Mic levels checked before key lines; phone-as-mic caught Keith once — prefer consistent capture for dock vs. fish house.
- **Safety:** Any powered vehicle shots: helmets, adult supervision, clear staging; match local regulations and property rules.

## Open items (non-blocking)

- Final box contents list and brand-safe B-roll.
- Exact schedule for dock vs. fish house segments (order of day).
- If “camel” in the transcript was voice-to-text for **camo**, align wording on thumbnails/titles with what Keith actually wants to emphasize.
